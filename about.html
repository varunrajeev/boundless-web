About us

At boundless.io, we know firsthand that building a startup is chaotic enough without getting bogged down by repetitive, manual workflows. You didn't launch a company to spend your days managing compliance checklists, migrating scattered data, or untangling administrative bottlenecks. That’s why our mission is simple: we build lightweight, powerful micro-apps that automate the mundane so you can focus on scaling the extraordinary. We act as a silent partner in your growth, giving you back the time and mental energy you need to actually build your business.

We believe that B2B software should just get out of your way. Instead of bloated, expensive platforms that take months to learn, we develop streamlined tools, like our flagship platform, Halo - that prioritize speed, simplicity, and compliance-by-design. Whether you're an agile team of two or a rapidly growing operation, our apps integrate seamlessly into your day-to-day routine to eliminate operational friction. We sweat the small stuff and automate the heavy lifting, so your team can stay focused on the big picture.
